About Chic

Home décor and styling has always been our passion. We love decorating and styling our homes. We strongly believe that designing house or office spaces is not always spending lavish amount of money on luxurious interiors and artefacts. But to us, it is more about making smart yet elegant and subtle changes that enhance the character of the place and its people… it is all about maximizing the space it offers without compromising on its inherent beauty and elegance. The more we think about it, the more we apply, the more we fall in love with home décor and styling. We find it to be such a great way to apply our creative minds and make our homes unique and comfortable to live in. We are passionate about design and interior related things and are always fine-tuning our homes. So when family and friends started consulting us for ideas on home styling, we thought it’s time to take a step ahead and do what we love to do but on a larger scale. This gave us a great opportunity to hone our talent and passion. Thus Chic Stylishly Simple was born.
Chic collections are elegantly and stylishly fashionable yet very simple and unique. Each and every piece of our collection right from the selection of fabrics to embellishments, everything has been carefully hand-picked by us and is hand-made. It’s full of different styles like modern, classic, edgy, and contemporary yet there is a very strong Indianness to it.
